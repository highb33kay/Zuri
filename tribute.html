Mummy Daramola

A Mother, A Teacher, A Friend, A Wife.

Mrs Daramola was a teacher and a friend, she taught us the value of love and the importance of compassion. Her words came easy, like gentle breeze just before dawn

She was married to Daramola and had 4 children. She lived in Lagos until her death in the year 2022. Her favourite phrase was, "o ti wa ri ni sin".

She spent a better part of her years teaching before and after she met her husband. She spent the latter part of her years reading and writing. i
